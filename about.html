About the Resource Kit

This resource kit covers the controversial aspects of gaming and how this has the potential to directly affect society itself.

More specifically, this kit includes detailed research about how video games can have an affect on the human brain, with particular attention to cognitive enhancement, mental health and addiction. Other common issues regarding a collective effect on society include capitalism and journalism through video games.
